About Me

I am in my senior year at Penn State Behrend majoring in Digital Media, Arts, and Technology (DIGIT).

I have a passion for digital creation, and I'm currently working as a student research assistant at the Virtual/Augmented Reality and Interactive Arts (VARIA) Lab located in the Lilley Library at Penn State Behrend.

Ever since I was young, I've had a fascination with video games, which was expressed mainly through playing them. Eventually this would lead to a desire to further appreciate and understand what goes into making a game. I have since learned game development skills through the courses found at Behrend.

I am constantly looking for opportunities to enhance my abilities, aiming to thrive in an environment that prioritizes innovation, creativity, and streamlined solutions. I would be excited to contribute my expertise to any role or business venture.
